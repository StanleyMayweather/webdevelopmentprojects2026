The Best Movies According to Stanley the Hybrid Of Lagos

My Top 3 Movies of all-time.

The Social Network

The Social Network is a 2010 American biographical drama film directed by David Fincher and written by Aaron Sorkin. It is based on the book "The Accidental Billionaires" by Ben Mezrich, which chronicles the founding of Facebook and the resulting lawsuits. The film stars Jesse Eisenberg as Mark Zuckerberg, the co-founder of Facebook, along with Andrew Garfield, Justin Timberlake, and Armie Hammer in supporting roles. The Social Network received critical acclaim for its screenplay, direction, and performances, and it won several awards, including three Academy Awards.

Jobs

Jobs is a 2013 American biographical drama film directed by Aaron Sorkin and written by Sorkin and Steve Jobs. It is based on the book "Steve Jobs" by Walter Isaacson, which chronicles the life of Steve Jobs, the co-founder and former CEO of Apple Inc. The film stars Ashton Kutcher as Steve Jobs, along with Rooney Mara, Brian Cox, and Katherine Waterston in supporting roles. Jobs received critical acclaim for its screenplay, direction, and performances, and it won several awards, including two Academy Awards.

The Wolf Of Wall Street

The Wolf of Wall Street is a 2013 American biographical black comedy drama film directed by Martin Scorsese and written by Terence Malick and Jordan Belfort. It is based on the book "The Wolf of Wall Street" by Jordan Belfort, which chronicles his time as a stockbroker on Wall Street. The film stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Jordan Belfort, along with Jonah Hill, Margot Robbie, and Matthew McConaughey in supporting roles. The Wolf of Wall Street received critical acclaim for its screenplay, direction, and performances, and it won several awards, including two Academy Awards.
